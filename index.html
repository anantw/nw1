About Nuin Wellness

Where Precision Science
Meets Natural Wellness

Founded in 2020 in Pune, India, Nuin Wellness Pvt Ltd was built on a singular conviction — that premium health outcomes require both rigorous science and nature's finest ingredients. We are a nutraceutical company operating at the intersection of evidence-based research and functional wellness innovation.

Our formulations are developed with clinically researched ingredients, adhering to international quality standards. From preventive nutrition to therapeutic-grade solutions, every product we create is a commitment to efficacy, safety, and transparency.
